Depression

level 3: Effects

Symptoms vary from person to person, as well as impact.

Each type of depression has their own diagnosis, but common symptoms of these types of depression include:

  1. Sleep: too little or too much, restless sleep
  2. Lack of concentration, suicidal thoughts, fatigue
  3. Behaviorally: Excessive crying, irritability, agitation, social isolation
  4. Changes in appetite, such as loss of appetite
  5. Rumination (repeatedly going over negative thoughts of one's distress)
  6. Loss of interest in things one normally does
