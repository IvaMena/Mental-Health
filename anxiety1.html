Signs of Anxiety and its Causes

Well here's the deal:

Anxiety can come from many things. It can occur do to the things around you, stress from jobs, schools, traumatic experience, a shortage of oxygen and many more factors. It can also be a sideffect from certian types of medication, stress from a medical condition, or withdrawl from substances. Anxiety can also simply come from genetics much like many other disorders.

Here's some signs:

Panic, fear, and uneasiness || Problems involving sleep / fatigue || Lack of concentration || Trembling || Irregular/increased heartbeat || Dizziness || Avoiding social situations in fear of getting judged || Nightmares || Worrying over things you know that are strange to worry over ||
