Depression

level 4: Help and Treatment Options

A person with depression behaves remarkably similar to a person who has a disease such as a flu or a cold (isolation and too little or too much sleep) It's been theorized that the evolutionary purpose of depression is to prepare one for being inflicted with an infection and the like. While there is chemical imbalance in the brain with depression (a depletion in serotonin) there may be a depletion of anti-imflammitory cells as well that cause the brain to become physically damaged. Dha (also known as omega-3 fatty acid) helps counteract this.

Therapy

It's also important for friends and family to support those who are going through depression of any kind. Going to a therapist or psychologist helps as well.

Support

Depression IS curable, and it requires patience and time to heal. You may lapse into depressive episodes at times, and that's normal for those suffering. Of course, it doesn't hurt to talk to a therapist, doctor, friend, and/or family member about your feelings. No one chooses to have depression or any other disorder.
