Depression

level 4: Help and Treatment Options

Therapy

It's also important for friends and family to support those who are going through depression of any kind. Going to a therapist or psychologist helps as well.

Support

Depression IS curable, and it requires patience and time to heal. You may lapse into depressive episodes at times, and that's normal for those suffering. Of course, it doesn't hurt to have a therapist, doctor, friend, and/or family member by your side most of the time. No one chooses to have depression or any other disorder.
