Depression

Level 1: Signs and Causes

There are multiple types of depression, each with their own range of severity:

  1. Persistent Depressive Disorder (formerly known as dysthymia)
  2. Major Depression (also known as clinical depression)
  3. Premenstrual Dysphoric Disorder (unique to anyone with a typical set of female reproductive organs)
  4. Bipolar Disorder (the really low end meets the criteria for Major Depression)
